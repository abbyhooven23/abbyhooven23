Bankers University Media Guide

For my sports communication class, my group created a media guide for a fictional sports team at a fictional university. My team spent the semester generating content for the competitive Monopoly team at Bankers University.

I assisted in writing each piece. I pieced the entire media guide together on Adobe InDesign and created the graphics.
